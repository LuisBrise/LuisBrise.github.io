I served as a teaching assistant for this undergraduate electromagnetic phenomena course, supporting students in understanding concepts in electromagnetism.

## Responsibilities

* Graded homework assignments and exams
* Hosted office hours for student consultation
* Led recitation sessions to reinforce lecture material
* Prepared detailed assignment solutions and study materials

## Course Topics

The course covered foundational electromagnetic theory including electrostatics, magnetostatics, electromagnetic induction, and Maxwell's equations, with applications relevant to Earth sciences.
